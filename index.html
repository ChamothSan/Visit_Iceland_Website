Reykjanes Peninsula

The Reykjanes Peninsula is a geological wonder, where lighthouses outnumber villages. It is home to Keflavík International Airport and, just minutes away, the spectacular Blue Lagoon. Reykjanes is also the youngest part of Iceland, having experienced eight eruptions since year 2023. Not to worry though, air traffic to and from Iceland was not affected by the eruptions and their impact was highly localized.
